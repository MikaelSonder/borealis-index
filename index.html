About Borealis Index

Borealis Index SRL is a Romania-based business services company focused on professional visibility, informational publishing, business profiling and reference environment development.

The company supports professional firms, consultants, specialized service providers and organizations operating across international commercial environments.

Services are delivered on a project basis and may include publication support, profile development, informational record maintenance and business visibility initiatives.
