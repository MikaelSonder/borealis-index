Borealis Index SRL

Borealis Index SRL is a Romania-based business information and cross-border visibility services company supporting professional service providers, advisory firms, consultants and specialized businesses operating in international markets.

The company develops and maintains digital reference environments, business visibility records and informational publication structures intended to facilitate international discoverability and professional positioning across jurisdictions and commercial sectors.

Business Activities

Services may include business profiling, professional visibility services, digital publication initiatives, directory inclusion, reference record maintenance, informational publishing and related business support services for organizations engaged in cross-border commercial activity.
